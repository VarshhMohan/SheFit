Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some FAQs to help you out!

1. What are SheFit's operating hours?

We are open seven days a week. Our regular hours are from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM on weekdays and from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM on weekends

2. Is personal training sessions offered?

Yes, we have a team of of certified personal trainers ready to help you reach your fitness goals. You can book one-on-one sessions starting at 500Rs per hour or opt for a package deal

3. What safety measures does SheFit have in place?

We take your safety very seriously. We've implemented enhanced cleaning protocols, and have trained helpers available to help in case of accidents with equipments and/or inquries.

4. Are there any group fitness classes available?

We offer a variety of fun and challenging group classes, including yoga, zumba and pilates. Check out our page for more details.

5. Can a person sign up for more than one sport/activity?

Absolutely! we encourage our customers to try out all the exercise sessions we provide and you can absolutely switch between different activities.
